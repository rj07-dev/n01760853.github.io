EMT Features

These tools help users build stronger resumes, target job roles more effectively, and improve their chances of getting noticed by recruiters.

Find a Feature

Search words like resume, ATS, formatting, jobs, keywords, or feedback.

Resume Customization

Users can change resume content so it better matches different job titles, industries, and employer expectations.

Field-Specific Suggestions

EMT gives guidance based on the type of field a person is applying to, such as tech, business, healthcare, or design.

Professional Formatting

The platform encourages a cleaner structure so resumes are easier to read and look more professional to recruiters.

Keyword Matching

Users can compare their resume wording with job descriptions to improve relevance and increase their chances of being noticed.

Improvement Guidance

EMT provides guidance on what to improve, including stronger wording and quantitative examples that show employers real impact.

Upload or Write Resume

Users can either upload a text file or write their resume directly on the website for quick editing and improvement.
