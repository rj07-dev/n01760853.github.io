# EMT Website

EMT is a front-end website project designed to support students, new graduates, and young professionals in the job search process. The platform focuses on helping users improve their resumes, highlight their strengths, and better prepare for recruiter and ATS screening. [file:1]

## Project Overview

The purpose of EMT is to make job recruitment feel more accessible by giving users tools and guidance to strengthen their resumes. The website includes multiple pages that present the platform’s features, pricing plans, contact options, and overall mission. [file:1]
